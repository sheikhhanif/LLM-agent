Maram Humaid, is an Al Jazeera journalist in Gaza. Her home was hit in an Israeli airstrike on Saturday.  She lived in an apartment building with her husband, her eight-year-old daughter, and their infant son. When Hamas fighters broke through Israel’s fence on Saturday, everything changed for them.
